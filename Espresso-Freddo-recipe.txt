
Ingredients:
 - 1	Double Espresso 
 - 10	Ice Cubes 
 - Shaker 
 - Straw

Directions:
 1. Make a double espresso

 2. Get the ice cubes out of the freezer.
 
 3. Put 5-6 ice cubes into your shaker (or even a small jar) and add the espresso.
  
 4. Close your shaker with its lid. If you can't find it, just improvise..
 
 5. Shake well until all ice cubes melt.
  
 6. Get a big glass and add a lot of ice cubes.
  
 7. Add your tasty mixture.

 8. Finish with a straw and you're ready to go!
